**Event logistics AI** has evolved from simple scheduling assistants to comprehensive operational engines. **Dynamic scheduling algorithms** automatically resolve conflicts when speakers cancel or room assignments change. The technology saved organizers of a major European tech summit approximately 120 hours of manual rescheduling work when five keynote speakers changed their availability two weeks before the event.
