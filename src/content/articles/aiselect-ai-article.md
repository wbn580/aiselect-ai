## Mastering Prompt Engineering for Designers

**Prompt engineering for designers** represents a distinct discipline from general AI prompting. While casual users might type simple descriptions, design professionals structure prompts to control composition, lighting, material properties, and stylistic consistency. The 2026 Interaction Design Foundation curriculum now includes dedicated modules on prompt architecture, reflecting its importance as a core design skill.
