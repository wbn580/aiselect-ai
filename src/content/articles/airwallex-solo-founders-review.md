Opening a business bank account as a solo founder in 2026 is still painful. Traditional banks require in-person visits, paper forms, and 2-3 week processing times. Airwallex does it in under 48 hours online, and gives you something traditional banks don't: multi-currency accounts with mid-market FX rates.

## Onboarding

We opened an account for an Australian Pty Ltd company. The process:

1. Online application — 15 minutes
2. Identity verification via passport scan + liveness check — 2 minutes
3. Business verification via ASIC lookup (automated) — instant
4. Account approved — 32 hours

No branch visit. No paper. No waiting on hold.

## Features that matter for solo founders

**Multi-currency accounts.** You get local bank details in 11+ currencies (AUD, USD, GBP, EUR, HKD, SGD, JPY, CAD, NZD, CHF, CNY). Each comes with its own account number. A UK client can pay you in GBP to your UK account details — no SWIFT fees, no intermediary bank charges.

**FX rates.** Airwallex uses mid-market rates (the rate you see on Google) plus a 0.3-0.5% margin, depending on volume. Traditional banks mark up 3-5%. On a $10,000 USD payment, Airwallex saves roughly $250-470 compared to a Big Four Australian bank.

**Virtual cards.** Issue unlimited virtual Visa debit cards for subscriptions, ad spend, and team expenses. Each card can be locked to a specific merchant or category. This alone has prevented ~$400 in forgotten subscription charges in our testing period.

**Xero integration.** Automatic transaction sync with Xero. Multi-currency transactions are converted at the transaction-date rate and categorized automatically.

## What's missing

**No lending products.** Airwallex is not a bank (it holds an AFSL, not a banking license). It does not offer loans, overdrafts, or credit lines. You still need a traditional bank relationship for credit.

**No cash or cheque handling.** Digital-only. If your business receives physical cheques or cash, you need a traditional bank account alongside Airwallex.

**Limited customer support for free plans.** Chat support is responsive during Australian business hours. Outside those hours, expect 4-8 hour response times. The paid plans ($19-49/mo) include priority support.

## Pricing

- **Free plan:** 0.5% FX margin, 10 free virtual cards, 1% on domestic transfers
- **Grow plan ($19/mo):** 0.3% FX margin, 50 virtual cards, free domestic transfers
- **Scale plan ($49/mo):** Custom FX rates, unlimited virtual cards, dedicated account manager

## Verdict

Airwallex replaces 90% of what a solo founder needs from a business bank account — and does it with better FX rates and zero branch visits. Keep a traditional account for lending and cash handling. Use Airwallex for everything else.

**Rating: 8/10.** Best-in-class for international solo founders. Not a full bank replacement.
