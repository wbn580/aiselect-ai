Hostinger Horizons launched in early 2026 as an AI-powered website builder bundled with Hostinger's shared hosting plans. The pitch: describe your business in a sentence, get a complete website with copy, images, and a mobile layout in under 2 minutes.

We tested it for 30 days on three use cases: a service business landing page, a portfolio site, and a simple e-commerce storefront. Here's what we found.

## What it gets right

**Speed is real.** The initial generation took 47 seconds for a 5-page site with AI-written copy and royalty-free images. The output was coherent — not groundbreaking, but coherent. Headlines matched the brief. The layout was responsive across mobile and desktop without manual tweaking.

**The AI copy is usable.** We prompted "a Sydney-based dog grooming service specializing in anxious dogs" and got a homepage, about page, services list, contact form, and booking page. The tone was professional. No hallucinated prices or locations. The AI pulled relevant images from Hostinger's stock library — a golden retriever being brushed, a calm grooming room, a person with a dog on a leash.

**Hosting is included.** The $2.99/mo plan (48-month term) includes the builder, hosting, a free domain for the first year, and SSL. Comparable standalone builders (Wix, Squarespace) start at $16-25/mo without hosting.

## Where it falls short

**Customization hits a ceiling fast.** You can swap text and images, change colors, and rearrange sections. You cannot edit the underlying HTML/CSS. You cannot add custom JavaScript. If you need a calculator widget, a booking integration, or a custom API call — you're out of luck. This is a brochure-site builder, not a platform.

**E-commerce is minimal.** The "store" feature supports up to 20 products with Stripe checkout. No inventory management. No shipping calculator. No discount codes. If you're selling more than a handful of items, look elsewhere.

**Lock-in is real.** Sites built with Horizons cannot be exported. You cannot download the HTML and move to another host. You're on Hostinger for the life of that site. For a $2.99/mo product, that's acceptable. But know what you're signing.

## Who should use it

- **Service businesses** (dog walkers, cleaners, consultants) who need a professional web presence with zero technical overhead.
- **Freelancers** building quick landing pages for client pitches.
- **Anyone testing a business idea** who doesn't want to spend $500 on a web designer before validating demand.

## Who should skip it

- **E-commerce sellers** with more than 20 SKUs.
- **SaaS founders** who need custom integrations.
- **Anyone who might want to migrate** to a different host in the next 2-3 years.

## Verdict

Hostinger Horizons delivers exactly what it promises: a functional, decent-looking website from a text prompt, hosted and live in under 5 minutes. It doesn't replace a developer for anything beyond a brochure site. At $2.99/mo, the price-to-value ratio is exceptional for the target use case.

**Rating: 7/10.** Strong for brochure sites. Skip for anything requiring customization or scale.
